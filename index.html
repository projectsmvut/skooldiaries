About Us

Skool Diaries is the leading Edu-Travel company with K-12 services for Schools. A joint venture by Team Adventure Singh and Team Sculpt Eye, it has catered to some top schools in Chandigarh Tri-city and North India.

With over 100 Man Years experience we have catered to Over 100 schools and approximately 10,000 students have benefited from our services.

The services comprises of International certification trips, wilderness education on-campus programs, one day outings and in-house school camping which are designed for experiential learning and life skills education under CCE Curriculum.
